HOW TO PLAY

Controls

  • Jump: tap the left side of the screen
  • Duck: hold the right side of the screen
  • Avoid coins and birds — they end the run

Coins per run

  • Free Game: 1 coin per 1,000 pts
  • Pay Game · $0.01: 5 coins per 1,000 pts
  • Character level adds bonus coins per 1,000 pts (see Upgrades)

What is GC?

GC (Game Coin) is an on-chain ERC-20 token on Base. You can mint GC 1:1 from the coins you earn in-game via the + Mint GC button in the Collection. GC lives in your wallet and is required to upgrade characters.

Character upgrades

Burn GC on a character to gain XP and level it up. Each level boosts the coins and score you earn while playing as that character.

1 GC burned = 1 XP. XP is soul-bound to your wallet.

Leaderboard

  • Press Save record to leaderboard after a run to submit your best score
  • Only scores you submit count — your wallet ranks by your highest saved score

Daily check-in

  • Tap Check-in every 24h to earn +1 coin
  • Every 5-day streak gives an extra +1 coin on top
  • Miss more than 36h and the streak resets to 0
